Emotional Intelligence

What is EQ?

Understanding and managing your emotions as well as recognizing and responding to the emotions of others.

5 Key areas:

  • Self-awareness
  • Self-regulation
  • Motivation
  • Empathy
  • Social skills

How is EQ different to IQ?

EQ - Being smart with emotions or emotional capacity - it is acquired IQ - Being smart with numerical or verbal data - harder to acquire or change Why is EQ important? EQ is important because it shapes how we communicate and build relationships. These two aspects of life can have a big impact on our outcomes in both our personal and professional lives.
