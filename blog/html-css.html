Margin, Border, and Padding Explained

Understanding the concepts of Margins, Borders, Padding is important when manipulating HTML content. I will attemp to explain the basics involved. It is important to note, these three concepts encapsulate the content of the HTML.

  • Content - can be either txt or an image
  • Padding - clears an area around the content and is generally transparant
  • Border - goes around the padding and content
  • Margin - clears an area around the border and is transparant
